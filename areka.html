Areka

Ingredients

  • 4 cups brown flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 3 cups of milk
  • ½ cup honey
  • ½ cup ghee
  • 10 grains of pitted dates
  • ½ cup cream

Recipe

  1. Arekah yeast is activated by dissolving the yeast with a spoon of sugar in warm water, and placed in a cup until the yeast doubles in size due to its interaction.
  2. Knead the dough of Areika flour and dates, by mixing the flour, salt, milk and yeast prepared in advance.
  3. Leave the Arekah dough covered and in a warm place for 15 minutes until it ferments
  4. Bake the dough after placing it in the form of a disk in a greased oven tray, and the baking continues for a period of 25-30 minutes, at 200 degrees Celsius, until the surface of the disk becomes red.
  5. Crumble the Arekah dough in a plate, add honey and ghee to it, knead it well, arrange it in a serving dish and serve with more honey, and decorate with pitted dates and fresh cream.

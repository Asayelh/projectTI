Masoob

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of bread
  • 1 kilo of bananas
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cans of cream
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • pinch of salt

Recipe

  1. In a food processor, put the bread after slicing it, then add the bananas and chop the mixture well.
  2. Add half the amount of cream with two tablespoons of honey and mix again.
  3. Add the ghee, stir well, then spread the mixture in a serving dish.
  4. Distribute the rest of the cream, add a little honey, and serve.

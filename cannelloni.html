Cannelloni

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 2 cups of milk.
  • pinch of salt
  • Sugar sprinkle.
  • tablespoon vinegar.
  • Mozzarella cheese.
  • Cheddar cheese.
  • Masajeh Chicken.
  • 2 tablespoons of sauce.

Recipe

  1. Put the milk and flour together and mix well.
  2. Salt, sugar, eggs and vinegar are put in to treat the smell of eggs.
  3. The ingredients are mixed together well and the consistency becomes liquid.
  4. Leave the mixture for half an hour to rest.
  5. Bring the mixture and bring a non-stick pan.
  6. Part of the mixture is poured to form circles of cannelloni the size of a bowl.
  7. It is placed on a medium heat.
  8. The cannelloni, after it has color on both sides, is taken out on a wide plate and covered with a clean towel.
  9. The whole amount of cannelloni is done and each time it is covered so that it does not dry out.
  10. The cheese is mixed with the chickpeas.
  11. The cannelloni chips are stuffed with chicken and cheese filling.
  12. The cannelloni is rolled up and closed from the edges.
  13. A layer is placed at the bottom of the tray of bechamel.
  14. Stack chicken cannelloni in Chinese.
  15. Spread the cannelloni with the sauce.
  16. A layer of bechamel is placed on the surface.
  17. Cheddar and mozzarella cheese are placed on the surface.
  18. Enter the oven on the middle rack on a medium heat until the face turns red.
  19. They are taken out, cut and put into dishes.

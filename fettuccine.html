Fettuccine

Ingredients

  • Half a kilo of pasta.
  • Two pieces of chicken breast cut into strips.
  • 3 tablespoons of butter.
  • A cup and a half of mushrooms.
  • One teaspoon of garlic powder.
  • Salt and black pepper to taste.
  • Quarter of a teaspoon of white pepper.
  • Parmesan cheese.
  • Cooking cream.

Recipe

  1. The pasta is boiled in an amount of water added to it with salt.
  2. Put a saucepan on the fire with butter and leave until it melts, then add chicken slices and mushrooms and stir the ingredients well until they change color.
  3. Add garlic powder, salt, black pepper and white pepper.
  4. Pour the cooking cream over the ingredients and leave on the fire until the consistency is firm.
  5. The pasta is filtered from the water, added to the mixture on the fire, stirred and left until it boils.
  6. Ladle into serving dishes and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top, and serve blissful and healing.

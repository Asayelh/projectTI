Alhaneth

Ingredients

  • Meat: 3 kilos
  • Vegetable oil: a cup
  • Tomato: 2 tablets (cut into slices)
  • Onions: 2 (medium size, sliced)
  • Basmati rice: 3 cups
  • Garlic: 5 cloves (cut into slices)
  • Saffron: little
  • Sweet green pepper: 2 tablets (cut into circles)
  • Salt: 2 teaspoons
  • Black pepper: 2 teaspoons
  • Cumin: a teaspoon
  • Turmeric: a teaspoon
  • Hill: 4 grains
  • Lemon leaf: a teaspoon
  • Mixed spices: a tablespoon
  • Bay paper: 3 sheets
  • Cinnamon: Oud
  • Cloves: 3 pcs
  • Banana leaves: 3 sheets

Recipe

  1. Wash the meat well with water, then put it in a large bowl.
  2. Put the salt, black pepper, turmeric, mixed spices and cumin in a small bowl and stir them together.
  3. Add the previous set of spices to the meat and stir well until the meat is seasoned and covered well with spices.
  4. Put a little vegetable oil in a wide pan, then add the meat and stir well until it changes color and takes on a brown color.
  5. Soak rice in hot water for half an hour.
  6. Wash the lemon and banana leaves well until they are completely clean.
  7. Arrange banana leaves and lemon in the bottom of the pressure cooker.
  8. Add half of the onion slices and distribute them well.
  9. Add half the amount of garlic, tomatoes, and sweet green pepper.
  10. We distribute the meat over the vegetables, then add the rest of the onions, garlic, tomatoes and sweet green pepper.
  11. Cover the meat with banana leaf and lemon. Close the pressure cooker well, then put it on a low heat for an hour and a half or until the meat is completely cooked.
  12. We put an appropriate amount of water on the fire to boil the rice and leave it until it boils, then add the rice with a little vegetable oil and a small pinch of salt and stir it a little.
  13. Add the soaked saffron and distribute it, then leave the rice on a low heat until it is cooked.
  14. Put the rice in a serving dish, then arrange the pieces of meat on top, then decorate it with raisins, almonds, and fried pine nuts, and serve hot.

Slik

Ingredients

  • Cassetten short grain rice
  • Chicken kilo
  • Tablespoon salt
  • 1 Onion
  • 6 of cardamom
  • Cinnamon stick
  • 3 Bay leaves
  • 9 Mastic cloves
  • 1/2 Cup of milk
  • 3 Tablespoons of ghee

Recipe

  1. Wash the rice well and soak it in water for half an hour or more
  2. We prepare the chicken for boiling, wash the chicken well, put it in a wide saucepan and cover it with water, add salt and onions and let the pot boil over a strong heat until it boils and leave it on a medium heat, and remove the foam formed on the surface of the chicken
  3. Add the cardamom, qoufa, laurel, and half the amount of mastic, cover the pot and let it boil for 45 minutes until the chicken is cooked.
  4. Take out the chicken and put it on a wide tray or oven dish and set it aside
  5. Preheat the oven to a temperature of 180 degrees Celsius and roast the chicken in the oven until it becomes golden in color
  6. Half the boiled chicken broth and set aside
  7. We bring a stainless pot and add the rice from the soaking water and put it in the pot, add four cups of boiled chicken broth and let the rice cook until the broth absorbs, stirring from time to time
  8. Add half of the milk and let the rice cook quietly until the milk is absorbed
  9. Add the rest of the milk and leave the rice until the milk absorbs and becomes soft, taste the taste and add salt if needed.
  10. Put the stew in a serving bowl and spread the chicken pieces over it
  11. To serve, heat the ghee in a small frying pan with the mastic, then pour the hot ghee over the boil and serve immediately

Philly Cheesesteak Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 1 beef top sirloin steak(3 pounds), thinly sliced
  • 2 large onions,cut into 1/2-inch strips
  • 1 can condensed French onion soup, undiluted
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 package Italian salad dressing mix
  • 2 teaspoons beef base
  • 2 large red or green peppers,cut into 1/2-inch strips
  • 1/2 cup pickled pepper rings
  • 8 hoagie buns or French rolls, spllit
  • 8 slices provolone cheese

Recipe

  1. Combine the first 7 ingredients in a 6-qt. electric pressure cooker. Lock lid; close pressure-release valve. Adjust to pressure-cook on high for 10 minutes. Quick-release pressure. Add peppers and pepper rings. Lock lid; close pressure-release valve. Adjust to pressure-cook on high for an additional 5 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes; quick-release any remaining pressure. Press cancel.

  2. Place bun bottoms on ungreased baking sheets, cut sides up. Using tongs, place beef, vegetables and cheese on bun bottoms. Broil 3-4 in. from heat until cheese is melted, 1-2 minutes. Add bun tops; serve with cooking juices.

Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • yolk of six eggs
  • 3 quarters of a cup of white sugar
  • 2 thirds of a cup of fresh milk
  • A cup and a quarter of heavy cream
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of vanilla
  • 450 grams of cream cheese
  • Sweet biscuit sticks
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • 3/4 of a cup of brewed coffee

Recipe

  1. We mix the egg yolk with the sugar to dissolve, then put it in the milk and cook over a medium heat
  2. Stir the mixture well for a minute, until it boils
  3. Leave the mixture aside to cool, then cover it well and put it in the refrigerator for at least an hour
  4. We bring a bowl and beat the cream with the vanilla, then put the cheese with the egg yolk until it becomes smooth
  5. In another singing, we put the coffee and dip the biscuit sticks in it, arranging them in a distinctive way on the plate
  6. Put the cheese mixture on top, then add the whipped cream
  7. Repeat the layers, then sprinkle with cocoa
  8. Cover the mixture and put it in the refrigerator from four to six hours to hold the mixture, here and on the lips.

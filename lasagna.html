Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna slices.
  • 500 grams of minced meat.
  • A cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • Half a cup of shredded Parmesan cheese.
  • 2 medium size onions, chopped.
  • Two cloves of minced garlic.
  • A cup of tomato sauce.
  • A tablespoon of tomato paste.
  • A teaspoon of basil.
  • Pinch of salt and black pepper.
  • A little olive oil.

Recipe

  1. Add olive oil, carrots, bell pepper, zucchini, onion, and a pinch of salt, and stir for 9 minutes.
  2. Add the spinach, and stir, in stages, for about 3 minutes.
  3. Drain the canned tomatoes from the water from the can, then cut them into small pieces, and add them with basil, olive oil, garlic, salt and black pepper to a pan to prepare the tomato sauce.
  4. Cut cottage cheese into small pieces, then add the cooked vegetables.
  5. Put a little tomato sauce, then lasagna slices, then mix cheese and vegetables, followed by tomato sauce, then repeat the process in the oven tray, and repeat the process until the quantity is finished.
  6. Put the mozzarella cheese in the last layer, then cover the tray and put it in the oven for about 20 minutes.

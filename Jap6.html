Matcha latte

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp matcha tea powder
  • 1 tsp maple syrup or honey
  • 250ml milk of your choice, dairy-free works well, as does coconut milk

Recipe

  1. Put the matcha and sweetener into a mug, pour in 1 tbsp boiling water and stir to a thick paste.

  2. Heat the milk until steaming, then if you can, froth until foamy with a whisk or milk frother. Pour into the matcha for your very own, caf√© style latte

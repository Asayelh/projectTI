Aseeda

Ingredients

  • A kilo of dates (preferably diabetic)
  • 2 liters of water
  • 4 and a half cups of brown flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ stick of butter
  • pinch of black pepper
  • pinch of saffron
  • cardamom as desired

Recipe

  1. Put the dates in the blender with a little water and mix well until blended and homogeneous.
  2. Put the homogeneous date mixture in a saucepan on a low heat and add butter and brown flour to it until it reaches the boiling point, then beat the mixture with a hand mixer every ten minutes until it becomes firm and becomes like a dough.
  3. Add salt, saffron, black pepper and cardamom, mix well and serve hot.

Crostata

Ingredients

  • Three cups of fresh berries
  • 1 and a 1/2 cups and three tablespoons of white flour
  • 3 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • Â¼ cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of yellow cornmeal
  • 9 tablespoons of cold, unsalted butter
  • large egg yolk
  • 3 tablespoons of ice water
  • big egg white
  • Lemon ice cream for serving
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar, one egg white, beaten with two tablespoons of water

Recipe

  1. Mix the berries with three tablespoons of flour and fine sugar in a bowl and keep it aside
  2. We prepare the shelves of the first and third ovens in the oven while preheating the oven to a temperature of 180 degrees Celsius
  3. We mix the remaining flour, granulated sugar and cornmeal in an electric mixer, beating them well to make the texture like fine sand.
  4. Add the butter while continuing to beat, then put the egg yolk and water while continuing to beat to form a soft dough
  5. We add a tablespoon of water each time as needed, then we collect the dough in the form of balls and divide them into two equal parts
  6. We stretch each piece of dough into a disk and put them in the bowls
  7. Divide the berry filling between the two disks, leaving an indentation around the edges of each disk
  8. Sprinkle the remaining flour and sugar mixture over the berries
  9. Brush the dough with a mixture of egg whites, sprinkle it with sugar, and put it in the oven for half an hour
  10. We replace the pots in the oven racks and continue baking until the crust is red and bubbles rise over the filling and serve with ice cream.
